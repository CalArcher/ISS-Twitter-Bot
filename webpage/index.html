Hello!

This API gathers data from an API that tracks the ISS coordinates and generates volcano related data.

To get the data, go to https://iss-tweet-bot.herokuapp.com/data.json.

JSON Data:

latISS: The latitude of the ISS the last time it passed over a volcano

longISS: The longitude of the ISS the last time it passed over a volcano

volcanoLat: The latitude of the last volcano the ISS passed over

volcanoLong: The longitude of the last volcano the ISS passed over

volcanoName: The name of the last volcano the ISS passed over

passedOverVolcanos: A current list of all volcanos the ISS has passed over, and the number of times it has been passed over

top3: The most frequently passed over volcanos, and the times they have been passed over
